Designing feature stores for humans under deadline

The best feature platforms reduce cognitive load as much as they reduce duplicated pipelines.

Feature reuse only works when engineers can understand what they are reusing. Names, owners, and freshness guarantees are not decoration; they are the interface.

Make Ownership Visible

Every shared feature should have a team, an escalation path, and a retirement story. Anonymous features become permanent because nobody feels authorized to delete them.

Prefer Boring Freshness Contracts

Minute-level precision is less useful than an honest contract that says when a value arrives, when it expires, and what happens during backfill.

Write for the Next Launch

A good feature definition answers the questions a launch reviewer will ask: where did it come from, what population does it cover, and how does it fail?
